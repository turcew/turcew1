Agridera Fresh Market Green Onion Hybrids

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of green onion seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world.
 In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining resistance to common pests and diseases into superior green onion breeding lines and hybrids. Moreover, comprehensive efforts are being undertaken to develop varieties with enhanced shelf life and flavor.

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding is for length, firmness, green color, flavor, and resistance to environmental stress.

Agirdera Green Onion breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Greenhouse Green Onions, Open-field Green Onions, and Bush Type Open-field Green Onions.

Greenhouse Green Onion Breeding Program

This breeding is aimed for the European and Asian markets. For long stems and resistances to pests, nematodes, and fungal diseases.

Open-field Green Onions

This breeding is for North American and African markets. The main focus is on robust stems and resistances to drought and pests.

Open-field Bush Type Green Onions

This breeding is for Mediterranean markets. We breed thick and elongated stems with resistances to common diseases.

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
